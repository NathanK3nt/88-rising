Joji

Joji is a Japanese-Australian singer, songwriter, and record producer. He rose to fame in the late 2010s as a YouTuber and comedian under the pseudonym Filthy Frank, and later as a solo musician and one of the first artists signed to the 88rising label.

Rich Brian

Rich Brian is an Indonesian rapper and record producer. He rose to fame in 2016 with the release of his debut single "Dat $tick" and later signed to 88rising. He's known for his unique sound and style, blending hip-hop and R&B with Asian influences.

Higher Brothers

Higher Brothers is a Chinese hip-hop group from Chengdu. They rose to fame in 2017 with their debut album "Black Cab" and later signed to 88rising. Higher Brothers are known for their unique blend of Chinese and Western hip-hop, and their ability to seamlessly blend Chinese language and culture with their music.
